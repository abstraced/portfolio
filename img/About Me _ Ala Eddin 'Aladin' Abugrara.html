ABOUT ME

How I became a web developer

My interest for programming started while I was observing my wife coding, I was curious about those terminals full of lines of, what was for me then, gibberish! The idea of writing scripts that gets executed by the computer was next to witchcraft to me, naturally, I wanted to learn it!

While learning PythonI I stumbled on Javascript and I got immediately hooked! Every little improvement got me excited and kept me wanting to know more. It was like learning to play music all over again!

I went through numerous courses on-line cementing my knowledge of HTML5 and the respective frameworks and libraries. I also took a peek at back-end technologies with the goal of becoming a MERN stack developer

Looking back to all that Javascript , CSS, React, Node and whatnot I feel my feet getting wet standing on the edge of an ocean and I am going sailing!
